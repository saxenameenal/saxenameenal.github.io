About Me

Hi! I'm Meenal Saxena, a 2nd year university student studying Bachelor of Computer Science (Advanced) at the University of Adelaide.

I have been passionate about technology from a young age after I observed how heavily impacted our normal day-to-day lives are due to continuous advancements, from always keeping up to date with upcoming technologies to reading biographies of popular entrepreneurs, such as Elon Musk. A defining moment for me as a young high school student was seeing Musk give a guest lecture in Adelaide. Seeing him perform a speech regarding his Mars settlement plans allowed me to realise just how significant technology is and how much I would like to be a part of this continuously developing field.
My aim is to own a successful start-up so that I too can assist people and create an impact in the upcoming technology industry, by contributing to my society in an area I am most passionate about.
